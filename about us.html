About Us

Welcome to the home of The Hit Men UK. We are a top class wedding, party, and corporate events band based in London, delivering a high energy repertoire of classic and contemporary funk, soul, ska, and rock & roll. We will, without any doubt, pack out your dance-floor. ​

If you want your guests to have a special and unforgettable night, then we are the band for you. ​ We have gained a reputation as one of London's best party bands.

We have played numerous events in venues such as The Rivolli Ballroom, The Baglioni Hotel, The Churchill War Rooms, and Greenwich’s Old Royal Naval College Painted Hall. We have been hired by the BBC, The Mayor of Bromley, St Olave’s Grammar School, among many other happy clients.
